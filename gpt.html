Frequently Asked Questions - Japan Student Visa & Japanese Language Classes

The COE is a document issued by the Japanese Immigration Bureau confirming your eligibility for a student visa. Processing typically takes 1-3 months. We assist with the entire COE application process to ensure timely approval.

Required documents include a valid passport, visa application form, recent photos, COE, financial proof (bank balance of ¥1.5-2 million), academic transcripts and certificates, and language proficiency scores (JLPT or IELTS/TOEFL). Our team helps verify and prepare all necessary documentation.

After submitting the application with the COE at the Japanese Embassy in Nepal, processing typically takes 5-7 working days.

You need to show a bank balance of approximately 1.5 to 2 million JPY (around NPR 1.3-1.8 million) to cover your first year's expenses.

For programs taught in Japanese, JLPT N5 or higher is typically required. For English-taught programs, IELTS or TOEFL scores are needed. We offer JLPT preparation courses to help you meet these requirements.
