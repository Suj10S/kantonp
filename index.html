Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What are the entry requirements for studying abroad?

Entry requirements vary by country and institution but generally include academic qualifications, language proficiency test scores (like IELTS, TOEFL), statement of purpose, letters of recommendation, and proof of financial support. Our counselors will guide you through the specific requirements for your chosen destination and program.

How long does the visa process take?

Visa processing times vary by country. Typically, it takes 2-6 weeks for most countries after submitting your complete application. Some countries might have longer processing times during peak seasons. We recommend starting the process at least 3-4 months before your intended start date to account for any delays.

What languages do you offer training for?

We offer language training for English (IELTS, TOEFL preparation), Japanese (JLPT preparation), Korean (TOPIK preparation), and German. Our courses are designed to help you achieve the proficiency level required for academic and immigration purposes.

Do you provide scholarships guidance?

Yes, we provide comprehensive guidance on scholarships, grants, and financial aid options available for international students. We help you identify suitable scholarships, prepare application materials, and meet deadlines to maximize your chances of receiving financial assistance.

How much does your consultancy service cost?

Our service fees vary depending on the destination country and the services required. We offer transparent pricing with no hidden costs. Please contact us for a detailed consultation where we can provide specific information based on your needs.

Can I work while studying abroad?

Most countries allow international students to work part-time during their studies, typically 20 hours per week during semesters and full-time during breaks. However, regulations vary by country, and some programs may have restrictions. We provide detailed information about work rights for your chosen destination.
